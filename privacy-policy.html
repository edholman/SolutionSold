SolutionSold SMS Program Policies

Privacy Policy

Data Collection & Use

We collect:

  • Your phone number and appointment details solely to deliver confirmation/reminder messages.
  • Opt-in consent (via web form or SMS reply).

Data Sharing

No marketing/promotional sharing:

"No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services, such as customer service, is permitted. All other use case categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties."

Permitted sharing: Subcontractors (e.g., SMS service providers) may process data only to deliver messages.

User Rights

  • Access/correction: Contact us at info@solutionsold.com.
  • Deletion: Text "STOP" to opt out; data is retained only as required by law.

Policy Updates

Changes will be posted at http://solutionsold.com/privacy-policy.
